Bio

I've always been an adventrous person. My father gave me a dirt bike when I was 14, from then on, I knew I wanted to ride motorcycles. The love for motorcyles prompted me to race professionally. I placed 3rd during my 1st professional race. Riding is my favorite passtime. I've had numerous motorcycles over the years, which includes: Kawasaki and Honda. Riding gave me the opportunity to meet new people, and meet life long friends. Overall, my best experience was when I rode to Gatlinburg, TN.
